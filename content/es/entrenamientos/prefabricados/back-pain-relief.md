## Important Note

If you have severe or chronic back pain, please consult a healthcare provider before starting any exercise program. Stop if any movement causes sharp pain.

## El Entrenamiento

Este therapeutic sequence targets common back pain from sitting, poor posture, and muscle imbalances. We focus on gentle stretching and strengthening the muscles that support your spine.

### Calentamiento (3 minutos)

**Pelvic Tilts (1 min)**
- On back, knees bent
- Gently tilt pelvis, press low back to colchoneta
- Release to neutral
- 15 slow reps
- Warm up the spine

**Knee Rocks (1 min)**
- Knees to chest
- Rock gently side to side
- Massage low back
- 30 seconds

**Cat-Cow (1 min)**
- On all fours
- Very slow and controlled
- Find what feels good
- 10 cycles

### Lower Back Release (6 minutos)

**Knees-to-Chest Hold (1.5 min)**
- Hug both knees
- Hold 30 seconds
- Single knee, other foot down
- 30 seconds each side

**Supine Figure-4 (2 min)**
- Ankle on opposite knee
- Draw legs toward chest
- Stretches piriformis
- 1 minuto each side

**Supine Twist (2.5 min)**
- Knees to one side
- Arms out
- Hold 1+ minuto each side
- Don't force the stretch

### Core Strengthening (6 minutos)

A strong core supports your back.

**Dead Bug (2 min)**
- On back, arms up, legs tabletop
- Lower opposite arm/leg
- Return with control
- 10 each side

**Bird Dog (2 min)**
- On all fours
- Extend opposite arm/leg
- Hold 5 seconds
- 8 each side

**Modified Plank (2 min)**
- Forearms and knees
- Or full plank if comfortable
- Hold 20-30 seconds
- 3 sets

### Hip Flexor Release (3 minutos)

Tight hip flexors pull on your lower back.

**Kneeling Hip Flexor Stretch (2 min)**
- Lunge position, back knee down
- Tuck pelvis, lean forward slightly
- Feel stretch in front of hip
- 1 minuto each side

**Prone Quad Stretch (1 min)**
- Lie on stomach
- Bend knee, hold foot
- 30 seconds each side

### Enfriamiento (2 minutos)

**Child's Pose**
- Knees wide or together
- Rest forehead down
- Arms extended or by sides
- 1 minuto

**Lying Down**
- Knees bent, feet flat
- Let low back be natural
- 1 minuto of breathing

## Daily Habits for a Healthy Back

- Take breaks from sitting every 30 min
- Walk daily
- Strengthen your core regularly
- Stretch hip flexors
- Sleep with pillow under knees if on back
- Lift with your legs, not your back

## When to Seek Help

See a professional if:
- Pain is severe or getting worse
- Numbness or tingling in legs
- Pain after an injury
- Pain disrupts sleep
- Bowel or bladder changes
