## El Entrenamiento

Start your week strong! Este energizing workout will wake up your body and mind, setting a positive tone for the days ahead.

### Energizing Warm-Up (3 minutos)

**Standing March (1 min)**
- High knees, pumping arms
- Get your heart rate up
- Set your intention for the week

**Arm Circles (1 min)**
- Big circles forward 15
- Big circles backward 15
- Wake up shoulders

**Standing Cat-Cow (1 min)**
- Hands on thighs
- Round and arch standing
- 10 cycles

### Power Core (6 minutos)

**Hundred (1.5 min)**
- Full hundred
- Pump those arms
- Legs at 45 degrees or tabletop
- 100 pumps

**Roll Up (1.5 min)**
- Arms overhead
- Roll up with control
- Roll down with control
- 8 reps

**Single Leg Stretch (1.5 min)**
- Alternate legs
- Keep upper body lifted
- 20 total (10 each side)

**Criss Cross (1.5 min)**
- Twist with power
- Elbow to opposite knee
- 20 total (10 each side)

### Strength Building (8 minutos)

**Plank to Downward Dog (2 min)**
- Start in plank
- Pike hips up
- Return to plank
- 10 reps

**Push-Up Series (2 min)**
- 10 regular push-ups
- 5 narrow (triceps)
- 5 wide (chest)
- Modify on knees if needed

**Swimming (2 min)**
- On stomach
- Alternate arm/leg lifts
- 40 counts
- Like you're swimming

**Side Plank (2 min)**
- 30 seconds each side
- Rest 15 seconds
- Repeat

### Lower Body Power (3 minutos)

**Bridge with March (1.5 min)**
- Hold bridge
- March one leg at a time
- 20 marches

**Glute Pulses (1.5 min)**
- Stay in bridge
- Small pulses up
- 30 pulses

## Monday Mindset

As you move:
- Set your intentions for the week
- Visualize your goals
- Feel yourself getting stronger
- You've got this!

## Make Monday Your Day

- Do this workout first thing
- Before the week gets busy
- You'll feel accomplished before 9am
- The rest of the week benefits from this energy

## Tips for Consistency

- Lay out workout clothes Sunday night
- Set an alarm 20 minutos earlier
- Don't check your phone first
- Make it non-negotiable
- Every Monday, same time
