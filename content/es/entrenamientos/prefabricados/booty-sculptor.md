## El Entrenamiento

Get ready to feel the burn! Este targeted glute workout uses Pilates principles to lift, tone, and sculpt your backside. Grab a resistance banda for extra challenge.

### Calentamiento (3 minutos)

**Glute Bridges (1 min)**
- Feet hip-width apart
- Squeeze glutes at top
- 15 reps, hold last one for 10 seconds

**Clamshells (2 min)**
- Side-lying, knees bent 90 degrees
- Open and close like a clamshell
- 20 reps each side
- Keep hips stacked

### Entrenamiento Principal (18 minutos)

**Side-Lying Series - Right Side (4 min)**

*Leg Lifts (1 min)*
- Top leg straight, flex foot
- Lift and lower with control
- 20 reps

*Circles (1 min)*
- Small circles, 15 forward
- 15 backward
- Keep hip stable

*Attitude Lifts (1 min)*
- Bend knee 90 degrees
- Lift from glute
- 20 reps

*Fire Hydrant Pulses (1 min)*
- Knee bent, lift to side
- Small pulses at top
- 30 pulses

**Side-Lying Series - Left Side (4 min)**
- Repeat all exercises on left side

**All Fours Series (5 min)**

*Donkey Kicks (1.5 min)*
- Knee bent 90 degrees
- Press heel to ceiling
- 20 reps each side

*Rainbow Arcs (1.5 min)*
- Straight leg, point toe
- Arc leg behind in rainbow shape
- 15 reps each side

*Fire Hydrants (2 min)*
- Lift knee to side, hip height
- 15 reps each side
- Add banda for more burn

**Bridge Series (5 min)**

*Single Leg Bridges (2 min)*
- One foot lifted
- Drive through standing heel
- 15 reps each side

*Wide Bridge Pulses (1.5 min)*
- Feet wide, toes turned out
- Small pulses at top
- 30 pulses

*Bridge March (1.5 min)*
- Hold bridge, alternate lifting feet
- Keep hips level
- 20 marches total

### Burnout (2 minutos)

**Glute Squeeze Hold**
- Bridge position
- Squeeze as hard as you can
- Hold 30 seconds
- Rest 10 seconds
- Repeat 3 times

### Enfriamiento (2 minutos)

**Figure-4 Stretch**
- Ankle on opposite knee
- Pull standing knee toward chest
- 45 seconds each side

**Pigeon Prep**
- Bent knee forward
- Back leg extended
- 30 seconds each side

## Using a Resistance Band

For extra intensity:
- Place banda just above knees
- Maintain tension throughout
- Don't let knees collapse inward
- The banda makes every rep count more

## Tips for Maximum Results

- Squeeze at the top of every rep
- Don't use momentum
- Mind-muscle connection is everything
- Do this workout 3x per week for best results
- Pair with protein for muscle building
