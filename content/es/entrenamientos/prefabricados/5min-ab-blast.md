## El Entrenamiento

Este quick ab blast is designed to fire up your core in just 5 minutos. Perfect for when you need a quick burn but don't have time for a full workout.

### Calentamiento (30 seconds)

**Pelvic Tilts**
- Lie on your back, knees bent, feet flat
- Exhale, press lower back into colchoneta
- Inhale, release to neutral
- 8 reps

### Entrenamiento Principal (4 minutos)

**Hundred Prep (45 sec)**
- Curl head and shoulders up
- Arms pump by your sides
- Legs tabletop or extended
- 50 pumps (5 breaths in, 5 breaths out)

**Criss Cross (45 sec)**
- Hands behind head
- Twist elbow to opposite knee
- Alternate sides with control
- 20 reps (10 each side)

**Double Leg Stretch (45 sec)**
- Curl up, hug knees to chest
- Extend arms and legs out
- Circle arms, hug knees back in
- 10 reps

**Scissors (45 sec)**
- Legs to ceiling, curl up
- Lower one leg, switch
- Keep hips stable
- 20 reps (10 each side)

**Plank Hold (45 sec)**
- Forearms or hands
- Body in straight line
- Engage core, breathe
- Hold 30-45 seconds

### Enfriamiento (30 seconds)

**Knees to Chest**
- Hug both knees in
- Rock gently side to side
- Take 3 deep breaths
- You're done!

## Tips for Maximum Burn

- Keep your lower back pressed into the colchoneta duaro supine exercises
- Don't pull on your neck duaro crunches
- Quality over speed - control every movement
- Breathe! Don't hold your breath

## Modificaciones

**Para hacerlo más fácil:**
- Keep feet on floor for hundred prep
- Smaller range of motion
- Skip the plank or do it on knees

**Para hacerlo más difícil:**
- Lower legs closer to floor
- Add ankle weights
- Hold plank for full 60 seconds
