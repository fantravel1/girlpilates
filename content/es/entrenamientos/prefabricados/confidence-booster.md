## El Entrenamiento

Before a big meeting, date, or whenever you need to feel powerful, this workout uses power poses and strong movements to boost your confidence through your body.

### Power Poses (3 minutos)

**Wonder Woman (1 min)**
- Stand tall, feet wide
- Hands on hips
- Chest lifted, shoulders back
- Hold 1 minuto
- Breathe powerfully

**Victory Pose (1 min)**
- Arms overhead in V
- Chin slightly lifted
- Feel yourself taking up space
- Hold 1 minuto

**Power Stance (1 min)**
- Feet hip-width
- Hands on hips
- Strong, grounded
- Hold 1 minuto

### Strong Core Work (6 minutos)

**Plank Hold (1.5 min)**
- Strong, solid plank
- Hold 30-45 seconds
- Rest, repeat
- You are strong

**Side Plank (2 min)**
- 45 seconds each side
- Stack or stagger feet
- Lift hips high
- Repeat

**Boat Pose (1.5 min)**
- Balance on sit bones
- Legs lifted (bent or straight)
- Arms reaching forward
- Hold 30 seconds
- 3 reps

**Swimming (1 min)**
- Strong, controlled
- 40 counts
- Feel your back muscles work

### Power Movements (4 minutos)

**Roll Up with Reach (1 min)**
- Roll up, reach past toes
- Roll down with control
- 8 powerful reps

**Shoulder Bridge Lifts (1.5 min)**
- Drive through heels
- Squeeze at top
- 15 strong reps
- Feel your power

**Push-Ups (1.5 min)**
- Any modification
- 2 sets of 10
- Strong through every rep

### Grounding Close (2 minutos)

**Standing Tall**
- Stand in mountain pose
- Arms by sides
- Shoulders back
- Head high
- Take 10 deep breaths
- Feel your strength

## The Science Behind It

Research shows:
- Power poses increase testosterone and decrease cortisol
- Physical strength translates to mental confidence
- Standing tall actually makes you feel more powerful
- Moving your body changes your brain chemistry

## Before Your Big Moment

Use this workout:
- Before job interviews
- Before presentations
- Before difficult conversations
- Before dates
- Before anything that scares you
- Whenever you need a boost
