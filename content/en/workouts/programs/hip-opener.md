## Overview

Tight hips are incredibly common, especially if you sit for long periods. This routine opens all aspects of the hips - flexors, extensors, rotators, and adductors.

## The Workout

### Warm-Up (3 minutes)

**Hip Circles on Back (1.5 min)**
- Knees to chest
- Circle knees together
- 8 circles each direction

**Figure-4 Rocks (1.5 min)**
- Right ankle on left knee
- Rock gently side to side
- Switch legs
- 30 seconds each

### Hip Flexor Release (5 minutes)

**Kneeling Hip Flexor Stretch (2 min)**
- Lunge position, back knee down
- Tuck pelvis under
- Lean forward slightly
- Hold 45 seconds each side

**Low Lunge with Twist (1.5 min)**
- From lunge, place opposite hand down
- Twist open toward front leg
- Hold 30 seconds each side

**Psoas March (1.5 min)**
- Lie on back, legs extended
- Slowly draw one knee to chest
- Lower with control
- 10 each leg

### Outer Hip & Glute (5 minutes)

**Supine Figure-4 Stretch (2 min)**
- Ankle on opposite knee
- Draw legs toward chest
- Hold 45 seconds each side

**Clamshells (1.5 min)**
- Side-lying, knees bent 90 degrees
- Open top knee, keep feet together
- 15 each side

**Side-Lying Leg Circles (1.5 min)**
- Top leg extended
- Small circles from hip
- 10 each direction, each side

### Inner Thigh (4 minutes)

**Seated Straddle (1.5 min)**
- Legs wide, hinge forward
- Keep back straight
- Hold 45 seconds

**Frog Stretch (1.5 min)**
- On forearms and knees
- Knees wide, feet together
- Gently rock back
- Hold 45 seconds

**Inner Thigh Lifts (1 min)**
- Side-lying, top leg forward
- Lift bottom leg
- 12 each side

### Cool-Down (3 minutes)

**Happy Baby (1 min)**
- Grab outsides of feet
- Open knees wide
- Rock gently

**Supine Twist (1 min)**
- Both knees to one side
- 30 seconds each side

**Constructive Rest (1 min)**
- Knees bent, feet wide
- Let knees fall together
- Breathe and release

## Tips

- Don't force stretches - ease into them
- Breathe deeply into tight spots
- Consistency beats intensity
- Do this routine daily if you sit a lot
