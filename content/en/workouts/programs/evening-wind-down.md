## Overview

End your day with this calming routine that releases physical tension and quiets the mind. Perfect for doing before bed to improve sleep quality.

## The Workout

### Breathing & Settling (3 minutes)

**Constructive Rest Position (3 min)**
- Lie on back, knees bent
- Feet flat, wider than hips
- Let knees rest together
- Hands on belly
- Deep, slow breaths
- Let each exhale release tension

### Gentle Spinal Release (4 minutes)

**Knee Drops (1.5 min)**
- Knees fall gently side to side
- Arms relaxed in T position
- Move slowly, breathe deeply
- 8 each side

**Pelvic Rocks (1 min)**
- Gently rock pelvis
- Arch and flatten lower back
- Tiny, soothing movements
- 10 rocks

**Supine Twist (1.5 min)**
- Both knees to one side
- Look opposite direction
- 45 seconds each side

### Hip & Leg Release (4 minutes)

**Figure-4 Stretch (2 min)**
- Ankle over opposite knee
- Let gravity do the work
- 1 minute each side

**Happy Baby (1 min)**
- Hold outer feet
- Rock gently
- Breathe into hips

**Legs Up (1 min)**
- Legs rest on wall or in air
- Let blood flow reverse
- Close eyes

### Full Body Relaxation (4 minutes)

**Body Scan Savasana (4 min)**
- Lie completely flat
- Arms slightly away from body
- Legs relaxed open
- Close eyes

Scan and release:
- Feet and ankles
- Calves and knees
- Thighs and hips
- Lower back and belly
- Chest and upper back
- Shoulders and arms
- Neck and throat
- Face and head

Let each area soften and sink into the mat.

## After the Workout

- Keep lights dim
- Avoid screens for 30+ minutes
- Consider herbal tea
- Journal or read something calming
- Transition gently to bed

## Sleep Tips

- Do this routine at the same time each night
- Keep bedroom cool and dark
- The body relaxation carries into sleep
- Even 5 minutes of stretching helps
