## Overview

Flexibility is essential for Pilates and daily life. This gentle workout focuses on lengthening muscles and improving range of motion throughout your body.

## The Workout

### Breathing & Centering (3 minutes)

**Constructive Rest (1.5 min)**
- Lie on back, knees bent
- Feet mat-width apart
- Deep belly breaths
- Let body settle into mat

**Arm Arcs (1.5 min)**
- Arms overhead with breath
- Sweep down to sides
- 6 slow reps

### Spine Mobility (5 minutes)

**Pelvic Curls (1.5 min)**
- Slow articulation up and down
- 6 reps

**Spinal Twists (2 min)**
- Knees side to side
- Arms in T position
- 8 each side, hold briefly

**Cat-Cow (1.5 min)**
- On all fours
- Arch and round slowly
- 10 cycles

### Hip & Leg Series (7 minutes)

**Single Leg Circles (2 min)**
- Small, controlled circles
- 8 each direction, each leg

**Figure-4 Stretch (2 min)**
- Ankle over knee
- Pull toward chest
- 45 seconds each side

**Happy Baby (1.5 min)**
- Hold outer feet
- Rock gently side to side

**Supine Hamstring Stretch (1.5 min)**
- Leg to ceiling
- Flex and point foot
- 45 seconds each leg

### Upper Body Release (5 minutes)

**Thread the Needle (2 min)**
- On all fours
- Reach under and through
- 45 seconds each side

**Chest Opener (1.5 min)**
- Lie on side
- Open arm like a book
- 45 seconds each side

**Neck Stretches (1.5 min)**
- Ear to shoulder
- Look up, look down
- 30 seconds each position

### Seated Series (3 minutes)

**Seated Forward Fold (1 min)**
- Legs extended
- Reach toward toes
- Relax and breathe

**Seated Straddle (1 min)**
- Legs wide
- Walk hands forward
- Hold 45 seconds

**Mermaid Stretch (1 min)**
- Seated side bend
- 30 seconds each side

### Final Relaxation (2 minutes)

**Savasana (2 min)**
- Lie flat, arms and legs relaxed
- Scan body for remaining tension
- Breathe deeply
- Let go completely

## Tips

- Never force a stretch
- Breathe into tight areas
- Hold stretches 30-60 seconds for best results
- Consistency matters more than intensity
- Do this routine daily for rapid improvement
