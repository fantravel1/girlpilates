## Overview

Pilates is incredible for upper body strength without bulk. This workout targets your arms, shoulders, chest, and upper back using your own body weight for resistance.

## The Workout

### Warm-Up (2 minutes)

**Arm Circles (1 min)**
- Arms out to sides
- Small circles forward 15 times
- Small circles backward 15 times

**Shoulder Shrugs (1 min)**
- Lift shoulders to ears
- Roll back and down
- 10 reps

### Push-Up Series (5 minutes)

**Wall Push-Ups (1.5 min)**
- Hands on wall, shoulder width
- 12 reps
- Focus on control

**Knee Push-Ups (1.5 min)**
- Traditional push-up on knees
- 10 reps
- Lower chest to floor

**Tricep Push-Ups (2 min)**
- Hands narrow, elbows close to body
- 8 reps
- Focus on back of arms

### Plank Series (5 minutes)

**Forearm Plank (1 min)**
- Hold steady
- Shoulders over elbows

**Plank Shoulder Taps (1.5 min)**
- High plank position
- Tap opposite shoulder
- 10 each side

**Up-Down Planks (1.5 min)**
- Forearm to hand plank
- Alternate leading arm
- 6 each side

**Side Plank (1 min)**
- 30 seconds each side
- Stack hips

### Arm Series Supine (5 minutes)

**Chest Fly Arms (1.5 min)**
- Lie on back, arms wide
- Bring arms together overhead
- Resist like pushing through water
- 15 reps

**Arm Circles Supine (1.5 min)**
- Arms to ceiling
- Small circles outward 15x
- Small circles inward 15x

**Tricep Press (2 min)**
- Hands behind head
- Press up toward ceiling
- 15 reps

### Cool-Down (3 minutes)

**Thread the Needle (1.5 min)**
- On all fours
- Reach one arm under body
- 30 seconds each side

**Chest Opener (1 min)**
- Lie face down
- Arms in T position
- Roll to open one side
- 30 seconds each

**Child's Pose with Arm Reach (30 sec)**
- Walk hands far forward
- Relax shoulders

## Tips

- Quality over quantity - slow movements build strength
- Breathe out on the effort
- Keep shoulders away from ears
- Modify as needed - all exercises can be adapted
