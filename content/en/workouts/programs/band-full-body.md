## Band Full Body Burn

This comprehensive workout uses resistance bands to deliver a reformer-style experience at home. You'll work every major muscle group with controlled, Pilates-based movements.

### What You'll Need
- Long resistance band (medium resistance)
- Loop band (optional, for lower body)
- Exercise mat

### Warm-Up (5 minutes)

1. **Band Pull Aparts** - Light resistance, 15 reps
2. **Band Arm Circles** - Arms extended, 10 each direction
3. **Band Side Steps** - Loop at ankles, 10 each direction
4. **Band Roll Down** - Holding band, articulate spine down and up x 5

### Main Workout (27 minutes)

#### Block 1: Upper Body Push & Pull (7 min)
- **Band Chest Press** - Lying supine, 12 reps x 3
- **Band Row** - Seated, 12 reps x 3
- **Band Shoulder Press** - Standing, 10 reps x 2
- **Band Tricep Extension** - Overhead, 12 reps x 2
- **Band Bicep Curl** - Standing, 12 reps x 2

*Rest 45 seconds*

#### Block 2: Lower Body Strength (7 min)
- **Band Squat** - Band at thighs, 15 reps x 3
- **Band Deadlift** - Standing on band, 12 reps x 2
- **Band Leg Press** - Supine, 12 reps each leg x 2
- **Band Clamshell** - Side lying, 15 reps each side x 2
- **Band Glute Bridge** - Band at knees, 15 reps x 2

*Rest 45 seconds*

#### Block 3: Core Integration (7 min)
- **Band Hundred** - Band around feet, 100 pumps
- **Band Roll Down** - With resistance, 8 reps x 2
- **Band Rotation** - Standing, 12 each side x 2
- **Band Side Bend** - Standing, 10 each side x 2
- **Band Teaser** - Band around feet, 6 reps x 2

*Rest 45 seconds*

#### Block 4: Posterior Chain & Shoulders (6 min)
- **Band Pull Apart** - 15 reps x 3
- **Band External Rotation** - 12 reps each arm x 2
- **Band Lat Pull** - Standing, 12 reps x 2
- **Band Monster Walk** - 10 steps each direction x 2

### Cool-Down (3 minutes)

1. **Band Hamstring Stretch** - Lying supine, 30 sec each leg
2. **Band Chest Stretch** - Band behind back, 30 sec
3. **Band Shoulder Stretch** - Cross body, 30 sec each
4. **Band Hip Flexor Stretch** - Kneeling, band assists, 30 sec each

### Tips for Success

- Maintain tension in the band throughout each exercise
- Control both the contraction and the release
- Keep core engaged to protect your lower back
- Choose appropriate resistanceâ€”challenge without strain

### Modifications

**Make it Easier:**
- Use a lighter resistance band
- Reduce reps to 8-10
- Take longer rest periods

**Make it Harder:**
- Use heavier resistance
- Add pulses at end of each set
- Shorten rest to 30 seconds
- Add isometric holds
