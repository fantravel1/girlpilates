## Overview

Feeling sluggish? This quick routine gets blood flowing, wakes up your muscles, and boosts your energy naturally. No mat needed - do it anywhere.

## The Workout

### Wake-Up Series (3 minutes)

**Breathing Activation (30 sec)**
- Stand tall
- 5 sharp exhales through nose
- Arms pump by sides
- Engage core with each exhale

**Arm Circles (1 min)**
- Big circles forward 15 times
- Big circles backward 15 times
- Feel shoulders warm up

**Standing Roll Down (1.5 min)**
- Roll down slowly
- Shake head "yes" and "no" at bottom
- Roll up slowly
- 3 reps

### Circulation Boost (5 minutes)

**March in Place (1 min)**
- High knees
- Pump arms
- Energetic pace

**Standing Hundred Arms (1 min)**
- Stand tall, slight knee bend
- Pump arms vigorously
- 50 pumps

**Side Bends (1 min)**
- Reach one arm overhead
- Bend to opposite side
- 10 each side

**Twist and Reach (1 min)**
- Feet hip-width
- Twist torso, reach opposite hand to knee
- 15 each side

**Squats with Arm Reach (1 min)**
- Squat down, arms forward
- Stand, arms overhead
- 12 reps

### Core Fire-Up (2 minutes)

**Standing Crunch (1 min)**
- Hands behind head
- Bring knee to opposite elbow
- 10 each side

**Plank Hold (1 min)**
- High plank or wall plank
- Strong and steady
- 45 seconds

### Finishing Burst (2 minutes)

**Jumping Jacks or Toe Taps (1 min)**
- 30 seconds moderate pace
- 30 seconds fast

**Calf Raises (30 sec)**
- Quick rises onto toes
- 20 reps

**Final Deep Breaths (30 sec)**
- Arms overhead with inhale
- Arms down with exhale
- 5 big breaths
- Feel energized!

## When to Use This Workout

- Mid-afternoon energy slump (instead of coffee!)
- Before a workout as warm-up
- Morning when you need a quick boost
- Before an important meeting or task
- Anytime you feel sluggish

## Tips

- Keep movements crisp and dynamic
- Breathe fully throughout
- Smile - it actually helps energy!
- Follow with a glass of water
