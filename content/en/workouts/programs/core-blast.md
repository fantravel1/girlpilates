## Overview

This focused core workout hits every part of your midsection - upper abs, lower abs, obliques, and deep stabilizers. Expect to feel the burn!

## The Workout

### Warm-Up (3 minutes)

**Breathing with Core Engagement (1 min)**
- Lie on back, knees bent
- Exhale, draw navel to spine
- Maintain engagement for 5 breaths
- Release and repeat 3 times

**Pelvic Curls (2 min)**
- Bridge up slowly
- Roll down one vertebra at a time
- Focus on articulation
- 8 reps

### Main Workout (15 minutes)

**The Hundred (2 min)**
- Legs at 45 degrees (or tabletop for modification)
- Curl up, pump arms
- Inhale 5, exhale 5
- 100 pumps total

**Roll Up (2 min)**
- Legs extended
- Arms overhead
- Roll up with control
- Roll down with even more control
- 8 reps

**Single Leg Stretch (1.5 min)**
- Curl up and hold
- Alternate pulling knees to chest
- Keep torso stable
- 20 reps (10 each side)

**Double Leg Stretch (1.5 min)**
- Knees to chest, curl up
- Extend arms and legs out
- Circle arms, pull knees back
- 10 reps

**Criss-Cross (2 min)**
- Hands behind head
- Twist elbow to opposite knee
- Fully extend other leg
- Hold each twist 2 counts
- 16 reps (8 each side)

**Spine Stretch Forward (1 min)**
- Sit tall, legs extended hip-width
- Round forward, reaching past toes
- Roll back up
- 6 reps

**Saw (1.5 min)**
- Arms out to sides
- Twist and reach opposite hand to outside of foot
- 10 reps (5 each side)

**Side Plank (1.5 min)**
- Hold each side 30 seconds
- Option: Lower knee for support
- Repeat each side

**Swimming (2 min)**
- On stomach, arms and legs extended
- Lift opposite arm and leg
- Flutter rapidly
- 3 sets of 20 counts

### Cool-Down (2 minutes)

**Child's Pose (30 sec)**
- Rest and breathe

**Spine Twist Stretch (1 min)**
- Gentle supine twists
- 30 seconds each side

**Knees to Chest (30 sec)**
- Hug knees, rock gently
- Release lower back

## Tips

- Quality over speed - control every movement
- If neck strains, keep head down with eyes on ceiling
- Exhale on the effort phase
- Stay connected to your center throughout

## Modifications

**Easier:**
- Keep head down during ab series
- Bend knees more
- Take breaks between exercises

**Harder:**
- Lower legs closer to floor
- Add pulses at challenging positions
- Minimize rest between exercises
