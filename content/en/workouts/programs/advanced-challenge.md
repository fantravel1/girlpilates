## Overview

This workout is for experienced Pilates practitioners who have mastered intermediate exercises. It includes complex movements requiring strength, flexibility, and precise control.

## Prerequisites

Before attempting this workout, you should be comfortable with:
- Full Hundred with legs at 45 degrees
- Roll Up with control
- Single Leg Stretch series
- Basic Teaser position

## The Workout

### Dynamic Warm-Up (3 minutes)

**Standing Roll Down (1 min)** - 3 reps with arm circles at bottom
**Hip Circles (1 min)** - Large controlled circles
**Spine Twist Standing (1 min)** - 8 each direction

### Hundred & Roll Series (5 minutes)

**The Hundred (2 min)**
- Legs at 45 degrees or lower
- Full 100 pumps
- Strong breath pattern

**Roll Up (1.5 min)**
- Slow and controlled
- 8 full reps
- No momentum

**Roll Over (1.5 min)**
- Legs overhead, parallel to floor
- Lower with control
- 5 reps

### Single Leg Series (6 minutes)

**Single Leg Circle (2 min)**
- Large circles, stable pelvis
- 8 each direction, each leg

**Single Leg Stretch (1.5 min)**
- Extended leg low
- 20 reps total

**Double Leg Stretch (1.5 min)**
- Full extension
- 10 reps

**Scissors (1 min)**
- Fast switches, legs long
- 20 reps

### Spine Series (5 minutes)

**Spine Stretch Forward (1.5 min)**
- Deep C-curve
- 6 reps

**Open Leg Rocker (2 min)**
- Balance and roll
- 8 reps

**Saw (1.5 min)**
- Deep rotation
- 5 each side

### Advanced Core (6 minutes)

**Teaser I (2 min)**
- Full teaser, hold at top
- 5 reps

**Teaser II (1.5 min)**
- Lower and lift legs only
- 5 reps

**Teaser III (1.5 min)**
- Everything lowers and lifts together
- 3 reps

**Corkscrew (1 min)**
- Legs circle overhead
- 3 each direction

### Hip Work (5 minutes)

**Side Kick Series (3 min)**
- Front/back kicks - 10 each leg
- Up/down - 10 each leg
- Circles - 8 each direction

**Bicycle (1 min)**
- 10 each direction, each leg

**Hot Potato (1 min)**
- Quick taps
- 8 sets each leg

### Swimming & Back Extension (3 minutes)

**Swimming (1.5 min)**
- Full swimming action
- 50 counts

**Rocking (1 min)**
- Bow position
- 8 rocks

**Swan Dive (30 sec)**
- 5 reps

### Finale (2 minutes)

**Seal (1 min)**
- 8 rolls with claps

**Push-Up Series (1 min)**
- 5 full Pilates push-ups
- Roll down, walk out, push-ups, walk back

### Cool-Down (3 minutes)

**Spine Twist Supine (1 min)** - 30 seconds each side
**Figure-4 Stretch (1 min)** - 30 seconds each side
**Final Relaxation (1 min)** - Full body scan, deep breaths

## Important Notes

- Stop if any exercise causes pain
- Modify as needed - even advanced practitioners have off days
- Quality always trumps quantity
- Stay connected to your breath throughout
