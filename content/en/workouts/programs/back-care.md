## Overview

This gentle routine is designed for those with back sensitivity or anyone wanting to maintain a healthy spine. Focus is on mobilizing, stretching, and gently strengthening the back.

**Note**: If you have diagnosed back conditions, please consult your healthcare provider before beginning.

## The Workout

### Gentle Warm-Up (3 minutes)

**Constructive Rest (1 min)**
- Lie on back, knees bent, feet flat
- Let knees gently fall together
- Place hands on belly
- Breathe deeply
- Let lower back relax

**Pelvic Rocks (1 min)**
- Very gentle pelvic tilts
- Focus on releasing tension
- 10 tiny movements
- No forcing

**Knee Drops (1 min)**
- Feet wide, knees together
- Let both knees fall to one side
- Only go as far as comfortable
- Return to center
- 5 each side

### Spinal Mobility (5 minutes)

**Cat-Cow (2 min)**
- Come to all fours
- Hands under shoulders, knees under hips
- SLOWLY arch back, look up (cow)
- SLOWLY round back, look at navel (cat)
- Move with breath
- 10 very slow cycles

**Thread the Needle (1.5 min)**
- Stay on all fours
- Reach right arm under body to left
- Let head follow
- Return
- Reach left arm under to right
- 5 each side, holding 3 breaths each

**Child's Pose with Side Stretch (1.5 min)**
- Sit back on heels
- Arms extended forward
- Walk hands to the right
- Feel left side stretch
- Hold 30 seconds
- Walk hands to left
- Hold 30 seconds
- Return to center

### Gentle Strengthening (5 minutes)

**Pelvic Curls (1.5 min)**
- On back, knees bent
- Peel spine off mat slowly
- Roll down even slower
- Focus on articulating each vertebra
- 6 slow reps

**Dead Bug Prep (1.5 min)**
- On back, arms at ceiling, legs in tabletop
- Lower right arm overhead while lowering left leg
- Keep back stable - stop if it arches
- Return
- Switch sides
- 8 total (4 each side)

**Swimming Prep (2 min)**
- On stomach, arms extended forward
- Lift right arm and left leg SLIGHTLY
- Lower
- Lift left arm and right leg SLIGHTLY
- Lower
- Very small movements
- 10 each side

### Cool-Down (2 minutes)

**Happy Baby (45 sec)**
- On back, grab outsides of feet
- Open knees wide
- Rock gently

**Supine Twist (1 min)**
- Knees to chest
- Let knees fall to one side
- Arms out, look opposite
- 30 seconds each side

**Final Rest (15 sec)**
- Legs extended or bent
- Hands on belly
- 3 deep breaths

## Important Tips

- **Never force movements** - Let your back guide you
- **Small is okay** - Tiny movements still help
- **Consistency matters** - Daily practice helps most
- **Stop if painful** - Discomfort okay, pain is not
- **Breathe** - Tension decreases with exhale

## When to Do This Workout

- Morning to ease overnight stiffness
- Midday break from desk work
- Evening to release daily tension
- After long drives or flights
- Any time your back needs attention
