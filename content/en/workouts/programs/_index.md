## Workout Programs

Choose from our collection of **17 expertly designed workout programs**. Each routine includes clear instructions, timing, and modifications for all levels.

### Quick Workouts (10-15 minutes)

Perfect for busy days or when you need a quick movement break.

- **Morning Wake-Up** (10 min) - Gentle energizing routine to start your day
- **Desk Break** (10 min) - No mat needed, perfect for the office
- **Energy Boost** (12 min) - Quick pick-me-up for afternoon slumps
- **Beginner Fundamentals** (15 min) - Master the basics
- **Back Care** (15 min) - Relieve tension and build strength
- **Evening Wind-Down** (15 min) - Relax before bed
- **Postpartum Recovery** (15 min) - Gentle core reconnection
- **Wall Workout** (15 min) - Only need a wall

### Standard Workouts (20-25 minutes)

Complete sessions for a satisfying practice.

- **Core Blast** (20 min) - Intense ab workout
- **Hip Opener** (20 min) - Improve hip mobility
- **Upper Body Tone** (20 min) - Arms, shoulders, and back
- **Posture Perfect** (20 min) - Stand taller
- **Prenatal Safe** (20 min) - Safe for all trimesters
- **Flexibility Flow** (25 min) - Deep stretching routine
- **Lower Body Sculpt** (25 min) - Legs and glutes focus

### Extended Workouts (30+ minutes)

For when you have time for a full practice.

- **Full Body Flow** (30 min) - Complete balanced workout
- **Advanced Challenge** (35 min) - For experienced practitioners
