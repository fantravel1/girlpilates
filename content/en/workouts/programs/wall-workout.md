## Overview

All you need is a wall! This workout uses the wall for support, feedback, and resistance. Perfect for hotel rooms, offices, or anywhere you can't roll out a mat.

## The Workout

### Wall Standing Series (5 minutes)

**Wall Stand Assessment (30 sec)**
- Back against wall
- Heels 2 inches from wall
- Notice: head, shoulders, tailbone contact?

**Wall Angels (1.5 min)**
- Back to wall
- Arms in goalpost position
- Slide up and down
- Keep contact with wall
- 12 reps

**Wall Squats (1.5 min)**
- Slide down into chair position
- Thighs parallel to floor
- Hold 30 seconds
- 3 holds

**Wall Push-Ups (1.5 min)**
- Face wall, arms extended
- 15 regular push-ups
- 10 tricep push-ups (elbows close)

### Wall Leg Series (5 minutes)

**Wall Plank (1 min)**
- Hands on wall, walk feet back
- Hold plank position
- 45 seconds

**Wall Single Leg Balance (1 min)**
- Back to wall, slight contact
- Lift one leg, hold
- 20 seconds each leg

**Wall Calf Raises (1 min)**
- Hands lightly on wall
- Rise onto toes
- 20 reps

**Wall Leg Circles (1 min)**
- Side to wall, one hand for balance
- Circle outside leg
- 10 each direction, each leg

**Wall Lunge (1 min)**
- Face away from wall
- Back foot on wall
- Gentle lunge
- 8 each leg

### Wall Stretch Series (5 minutes)

**Wall Chest Opener (1 min)**
- Side to wall
- Arm on wall, turn away
- 30 seconds each side

**Wall Hamstring Stretch (1.5 min)**
- Lie on back, legs up wall
- Scoot close to wall
- Breathe and relax
- 90 seconds

**Wall Straddle (1.5 min)**
- Legs up wall, let them open
- Gravity stretches inner thighs
- 90 seconds

**Wall Spinal Twist (1 min)**
- Legs up wall, lower to one side
- 30 seconds each side

## Tips

- Find a clear wall space
- Remove shoes for better grip
- The wall provides feedback for alignment
- Great for travel or office breaks
