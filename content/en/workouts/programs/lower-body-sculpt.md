## Overview

This workout targets all the muscles of your lower body - quads, hamstrings, glutes, inner thighs, and outer hips. Get ready to feel the burn!

## The Workout

### Warm-Up (3 minutes)

**Hip Circles (1 min)**
**Pelvic Curls (1 min)** - 8 reps
**Single Leg Circles (1 min)** - 5 each direction, each leg

### Supine Leg Series (6 minutes)

**Bridges (1.5 min)**
- Standard bridges, squeeze glutes at top
- 12 reps

**Single Leg Bridge (1.5 min)**
- One leg extended to ceiling
- 8 each leg

**Bridge Pulses (1 min)**
- Hold at top, pulse 20 times

**Hamstring Curls (2 min)**
- Bridge position, walk feet away
- Walk back in
- 8 reps

### Side-Lying Series - Right (5 minutes)

**Leg Lifts (1 min)** - 20 reps
**Front and Back Kicks (1 min)** - 15 reps
**Circles (1 min)** - 10 each direction
**Inner Thigh Lifts (1 min)** - 15 reps
**Clamshells (1 min)** - 15 reps

### Side-Lying Series - Left (5 minutes)

Repeat all exercises on left side

### All Fours Series (4 minutes)

**Donkey Kicks (1 min)**
- 12 each leg

**Fire Hydrants (1 min)**
- 12 each leg

**Leg Extensions (1 min)**
- Extend leg straight back
- 10 each leg

**Knee Circles (1 min)**
- 8 each direction, each leg

### Cool-Down (2 minutes)

**Figure-4 Stretch (1 min)** - 30 sec each side
**Quad Stretch (30 sec)** - Lie on side, pull foot to glute
**Child's Pose (30 sec)**

## Tips

- Squeeze the muscle you're working
- Don't rush - control builds strength
- Keep hips stacked during side-lying work
- Engage core throughout to protect lower back
