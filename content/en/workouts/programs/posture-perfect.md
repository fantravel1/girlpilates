## Overview

Poor posture affects most adults, especially those who sit frequently. This workout targets the muscles that support good posture while releasing those that pull us into poor alignment.

## The Workout

### Postural Assessment (1 minute)

**Stand Awareness**
- Stand against a wall
- Notice: Does your head touch? Lower back gap?
- This is your starting point

### Release Tight Muscles (4 minutes)

**Chest Opener (1 min)**
- Lie on foam roller or rolled towel along spine
- Arms wide in T position
- Breathe and let chest open

**Neck Release (1 min)**
- Gentle neck circles
- Ear to shoulder stretches
- 30 seconds each side

**Hip Flexor Stretch (2 min)**
- Kneeling lunge position
- Tuck pelvis under
- 45 seconds each side

### Strengthen Postural Muscles (10 minutes)

**Chin Tucks (1 min)**
- Lie on back, no pillow
- Draw chin toward throat
- Hold 5 seconds, 10 reps

**Chest Lift (1.5 min)**
- Hands behind head
- Lift upper back only
- Keep neck long
- 12 reps

**Swimming Prep (2 min)**
- Lie face down
- Lift opposite arm and leg
- Alternate slowly
- 10 each side

**Dart (1.5 min)**
- Face down, arms by sides
- Lift chest, squeeze shoulder blades
- Hold 5 seconds
- 8 reps

**All Fours Arm Reach (2 min)**
- Table top position
- Reach one arm forward
- 10 each side

**Plank (1 min)**
- Forearm plank
- Focus on alignment
- Hold 30-45 seconds

**Wall Angels (1 min)**
- Stand with back to wall
- Arms in goalpost position
- Slide up and down
- 10 reps

### Standing Integration (3 minutes)

**Standing Roll Down (1 min)**
- 3 slow roll downs
- Stack vertebra by vertebra

**Shoulder Blade Squeezes (1 min)**
- Stand tall
- Squeeze shoulder blades together
- Hold 5 seconds
- 10 reps

**Tall Stand (1 min)**
- Imagine string pulling crown up
- Shoulders over hips over ankles
- Take 5 deep breaths
- Notice the difference from start

### Awareness Practice (2 minutes)

**Posture Scan**
- Sit on edge of chair
- Feet flat
- Stack spine tall
- Relax shoulders down
- Draw chin slightly back
- Breathe

**Set Intention**
- Notice this aligned feeling
- Commit to checking in throughout your day
- Set phone reminders if needed

## Daily Posture Tips

- Set hourly reminders to check posture
- Adjust workstation ergonomics
- Take movement breaks every 30-60 minutes
- Strengthen back, stretch chest
- Consider standing desk options
