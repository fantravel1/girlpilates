## Ring Tone & Sculpt

The Magic Circle is perfect for toning and sculpting without heavy weights. This workout targets the areas women often want to focus on: inner thighs, arms, and core.

### What You'll Need
- Pilates Magic Circle (ring)
- Exercise mat

### Warm-Up (4 minutes)

1. **Arm Circles** - With ring in hands, small circles (1 min)
2. **Standing Balance** - Ring between palms, gentle pulses (1 min)
3. **Hip Circles** - Standing, ring at chest, circle hips (1 min)
4. **Shoulder Rolls** - Release tension (1 min)

### Main Workout (24 minutes)

#### Block 1: Upper Body (6 min)
- **Ring Arm Press** - At chest level, 15 pulses x 3
- **Ring Overhead Press** - Above head, 12 pulses x 2
- **Ring Behind Back** - Squeeze behind, 12 reps x 2
- **Ring Chest Fly** - Open and close at chest, 15 reps x 2

*Rest 30 seconds*

#### Block 2: Lower Body (6 min)
- **Ring Squeeze Standing** - Between thighs, 20 pulses x 3
- **Ring Squeeze Supine** - Lying down, 15 pulses x 3
- **Ring Inner Thigh Lift** - Side lying, 12 reps each side x 2
- **Ring Bridge** - Ring between knees, 15 reps x 2

*Rest 30 seconds*

#### Block 3: Core Classics (6 min)
- **Ring Hundred** - Ring between ankles, 100 pumps
- **Ring Roll Up** - Holding ring, 8 reps x 2
- **Ring Single Leg Stretch** - Ring around shin, 10 reps each x 2
- **Ring Teaser** - Holding ring, 5 reps x 2

*Rest 30 seconds*

#### Block 4: Full Body Finish (6 min)
- **Ring Seated Twist** - Rotation with ring, 10 each side x 2
- **Ring Plank** - Hands on ring, hold 20 sec x 3
- **Ring Back Extension** - Prone, holding ring, 10 reps x 2
- **Ring Arm Circles** - Standing, pressing ring, 10 each direction x 2

### Cool-Down (2 minutes)

1. **Ring Mermaid** - Side stretch with ring (30 sec each side)
2. **Seated Forward Fold** - Ring on floor, reach (30 sec)
3. **Chest Opener** - Ring behind back, gentle stretch (30 sec)

### Tips for Success

- Squeeze the ring with control, not force
- Keep shoulders relaxed and down
- Focus on the muscles working, not just the movement
- Maintain steady breathing throughout

### Modifications

**Make it Easier:**
- Use fewer pulses/reps
- Skip the advanced core exercises
- Use a lighter resistance ring

**Make it Harder:**
- Add isometric holds (squeeze and hold 5 seconds)
- Slow down the tempo
- Use a heavier resistance ring
