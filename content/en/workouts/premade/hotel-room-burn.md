## The Workout

Traveling doesn't mean skipping your workout! This routine uses just the floor and wall - no equipment needed. Perfect for that tiny hotel room.

### Warm-Up (2 minutes)

**Standing Movements**
- March in place (30 sec)
- Arm circles forward and back (30 sec)
- Hip circles (30 sec)
- Standing cat-cow (30 sec)

### Wall Work (5 minutes)

**Wall Sit (1 min)**
- Back flat against wall
- Thighs parallel to floor
- Hold 30-45 seconds
- Rest 15 seconds, repeat

**Wall Push-Ups (2 min)**
- Hands on wall, shoulder width
- Lean in, push back
- 3 sets of 15

**Wall Angels (2 min)**
- Back against wall
- Arms in goalpost position
- Slide up and down
- 15 slow reps

### Floor Work - Core (5 minutes)

**Plank Series (2 min)**
- Forearm plank 30 seconds
- Right side plank 30 seconds
- Left side plank 30 seconds
- Forearm plank 30 seconds

**Ab Series (3 min)**
- 20 crunches
- 20 bicycle crunches
- 20 leg lowers
- 10 roll-ups

### Floor Work - Lower Body (5 minutes)

**Glute Bridge Series (2 min)**
- 20 bridges
- 20 single leg bridges (10 each)
- 20 wide stance bridges

**Leg Series on Back (3 min)**
- 20 scissors
- 30 flutter kicks
- 20 leg circles each direction

### Floor Work - Arms & Shoulders (3 minutes)

**Push-Up Variations**
- 10 standard push-ups (or on knees)
- 10 narrow push-ups (triceps)
- 10 wide push-ups
- Tricep dips using bed edge: 15 reps

### Cool-Down (2 minutes)

**Stretches**
- Forward fold, hang 30 seconds
- Quad stretch each leg 20 seconds
- Hip flexor stretch each side 20 seconds
- Seated twist each side 20 seconds

## Space Requirements

You need approximately:
- 6 feet x 4 feet of floor space
- A wall to lean against
- That's it!

## Travel Tips

- Do this first thing in the morning before the day gets away from you
- Open the curtains for natural light and energy
- Use a towel as a mat if the floor is hard
- Pack a resistance band for added challenge
- Stay hydrated - hotel rooms are often dry
