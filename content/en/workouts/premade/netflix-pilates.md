## The Concept

Yes, you can watch TV and work out! This workout is designed for multitasking - exercises that don't require intense focus, so you can follow along with your show while getting your Pilates in.

## Setting Up

1. Roll out your mat in front of the TV
2. Queue up your show
3. Keep this page open on your phone for reference
4. Press play on both!

## The Workout

### During the Opening Credits (5 min)

**Warm-Up Moves**
- Lie on your back, knees bent
- 20 pelvic tilts
- 10 hip circles each direction
- 20 shoulder bridges

### First 10 Minutes of Show

**Core Series on Your Back**
- 50 Hundred pulses
- 20 single leg stretches (alternating)
- 10 double leg stretches
- 20 scissors (alternating)
- 20 bicycle crunches
- Rest during any exciting scenes!

### Minutes 10-20

**Side-Lying Series (each side)**
- 15 side leg lifts
- 15 small circles forward
- 15 small circles backward
- 10 inner thigh lifts
- 15 clams

### Minutes 20-25

**All Fours Work**
- 20 fire hydrants (10 each side)
- 20 donkey kicks (10 each side)
- 1 minute plank (or during commercials if streaming)
- 20 bird dogs (10 each side)

### Final 5 Minutes / End Credits

**Stretching**
- Figure-4 stretch (1 min each side)
- Supine twist (1 min each side)
- Child's pose (1 min)
- Happy baby (30 sec)
- Savasana through credits

## Pro Tips

- Pick a show you've seen before if you want to focus more on form
- Save new episodes for when you want to focus on the show more
- Reality TV and sitcoms work great - nothing too plot-heavy
- Pause during really exciting moments!

## Show Pairing Suggestions

**Light & Easy Shows for Focused Exercise:**
- Cooking shows
- Home renovation shows
- Reality competition shows
- Sitcom reruns

**When You Want to Focus on the Show:**
- Do more stretching and gentle moves
- Side-lying work is great for watching
- Save intense core work for commercial breaks

## Why This Works

- You're more likely to do something if it's enjoyable
- 30 minutes goes by quickly when you're entertained
- You'll associate your favorite shows with movement
- No more guilt about "just watching TV"

## Make It a Habit

- Same show, same time, same workout
- Build a Pilates playlist on Netflix
- Tell yourself "just one episode" to start
- You might find yourself wanting to do more!
