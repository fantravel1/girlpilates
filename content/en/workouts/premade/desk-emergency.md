## The Workout

That tension headache starting? Shoulders up by your ears? This 5-minute emergency relief sequence can be done right at your desk - no mat or special clothes needed.

### Seated Relief (3 minutes)

**Neck Release (1 min)**
- Drop chin to chest
- Roll right ear toward right shoulder
- Hold 15 seconds
- Roll through center to left
- Hold 15 seconds
- Repeat

**Shoulder Shrugs (30 sec)**
- Lift shoulders to ears
- Hold 3 seconds
- Drop and release
- 5 times

**Shoulder Rolls (30 sec)**
- Roll backward 5 times
- Roll forward 5 times
- Slow and controlled

**Seated Cat-Cow (30 sec)**
- Hands on knees
- Round spine, tuck chin (cat)
- Arch back, look up (cow)
- 5 cycles

**Seated Twist (30 sec)**
- Grab back of chair with one hand
- Twist toward that side
- 15 seconds each side

### Standing Relief (2 minutes)

**Standing Chest Opener (30 sec)**
- Stand up
- Clasp hands behind back
- Lift chest, squeeze shoulder blades
- Hold 20-30 seconds

**Forward Fold (30 sec)**
- Feet hip-width
- Fold forward, let head hang
- Hold 20-30 seconds
- Release neck tension

**Shoulder Stretch (30 sec)**
- Right arm across chest
- Left hand on right elbow
- Hold 15 seconds
- Switch sides

**Wrist Circles (30 sec)**
- Circle wrists 10 times each direction
- Shake out hands
- Open and close fists

## Quick Fixes Throughout the Day

**Every 30 Minutes:**
- Roll shoulders back 5 times
- Look away from screen (20-20-20 rule)

**Every Hour:**
- Stand up and stretch
- Walk to get water
- Do 5 deep breaths

**When Tension Builds:**
- Drop shoulders
- Unclench jaw
- Relax face muscles

## Desk Ergonomics Check

While you're at it, check your setup:
- Screen at eye level
- Feet flat on floor
- Keyboard at elbow height
- Shoulders relaxed, not hunched
- Take breaks!

## Prevention Tips

- Set hourly reminders to move
- Keep water at your desk
- Consider a standing desk option
- Use a good chair
- Position monitor to avoid looking down
