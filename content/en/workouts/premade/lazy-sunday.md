## The Vibe

No rush. No goals. Just you, your mat, and some delicious stretches. This flow is about feeling good, not working hard. Take your time with each position.

### Opening (5 minutes)

**In Bed or On Mat**
- Full body stretch - reach arms overhead, point toes
- Hold and breathe
- Hug knees to chest
- Rock side to side
- 2 minutes of gentle waking up

**Neck Releases**
- Sit comfortably
- Drop ear to shoulder, hold 30 seconds
- Roll chin to chest
- Drop ear to other shoulder, hold 30 seconds
- No rushing

### Seated Stretches (8 minutes)

**Seated Side Stretch (2 min)**
- Reach arm overhead, lean to side
- 1 minute each side
- Breathe into the stretch

**Seated Twist (2 min)**
- Easy cross-legged
- Twist to right, left hand on knee
- 1 minute each side
- Look over back shoulder

**Forward Fold (2 min)**
- Legs extended or crossed
- Fold forward from hips
- Let head hang heavy
- Hold 2 minutes, just breathe

**Butterfly Stretch (2 min)**
- Soles of feet together
- Knees open
- Fold forward if it feels good
- Hold 2 minutes

### Floor Stretches (12 minutes)

**Cat-Cow (2 min)**
- The slowest cat-cow ever
- 30 second holds in each position
- Really feel each vertebra

**Child's Pose (3 min)**
- Knees together or wide
- Arms extended or by sides
- Just rest here
- This is your Sunday sanctuary

**Pigeon Pose (4 min)**
- 2 minutes each side
- Use a pillow under hip if needed
- Fold forward when ready
- This is where we hold stress

**Supine Twist (3 min)**
- Arms out wide
- Knees to one side
- Hold 1.5 minutes each side
- Let gravity do the work

### Closing (5 minutes)

**Legs Up the Wall**
- Or legs on couch/bed
- Arms out or on belly
- Close your eyes
- 3-5 minutes of pure bliss
- You don't have to do anything

## Sunday Self-Care Ideas

Before your flow:
- Make a cup of tea or coffee
- Put on cozy clothes
- Light a candle
- Put on soft music

After your flow:
- Take a bath
- Read a book
- Nap if you want
- Maintain the peaceful vibe

## The Rules of Lazy Sunday Flow

1. There are no rules
2. Skip anything that doesn't feel good
3. Stay longer in poses you love
4. No judgment, no goals
5. This is YOUR time
