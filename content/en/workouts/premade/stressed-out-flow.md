## The Workout

When stress has you wound tight, this gentle flow will help release tension and calm your nervous system. Focus on deep breathing throughout.

### Centering (2 minutes)

**Constructive Rest**
- Lie on your back, knees bent
- Feet wider than hips, knees falling together
- Hands on belly
- 10 deep breaths - inhale 4 counts, exhale 6 counts

### Main Flow (11 minutes)

**Neck Releases (2 min)**
- Drop right ear to right shoulder
- Hold 30 seconds
- Small circles with chin
- Repeat left side

**Shoulder Rolls (1 min)**
- Roll shoulders up, back, down
- 10 rolls backward
- 5 rolls forward

**Supine Spinal Twist (2 min)**
- Arms out to sides
- Knees to chest, drop to right
- Look left
- Hold 1 minute each side
- Breathe into the stretch

**Cat-Cow (2 min)**
- All fours position
- Inhale, arch back, look up (cow)
- Exhale, round spine, tuck chin (cat)
- 15 slow cycles
- Follow your breath

**Child's Pose with Side Stretch (2 min)**
- Sit back onto heels
- Walk hands to right, stretching left side
- Hold 45 seconds
- Walk through center to left side
- Hold 45 seconds

**Thread the Needle (2 min)**
- From all fours
- Thread right arm under body
- Rest right shoulder and head on mat
- Hold 45 seconds
- Repeat left side

### Cool-Down (2 minutes)

**Legs Up the Wall** (or resting on a chair)
- Scoot hips to wall
- Legs extended up
- Arms relaxed by sides
- Close your eyes
- Stay 2 minutes
- Let gravity release tension

## Breathing Guide

Throughout this flow:
- Inhale through your nose for 4 counts
- Exhale through your nose (or mouth) for 6 counts
- The longer exhale activates your parasympathetic nervous system
- If your mind wanders, gently return to your breath

## When to Do This Flow

- After a stressful day at work
- When you can't fall asleep
- During a difficult week
- Whenever you feel overwhelmed
- As a calming break between meetings

## Create a Calming Space

- Dim the lights or use candles
- Play soft music or nature sounds
- Use a lavender eye pillow
- Keep the room warm
- Turn off your phone notifications
