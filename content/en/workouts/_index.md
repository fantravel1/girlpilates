### How to Choose Your Workout

1. **Consider your time** - 10 minutes or 30?
2. **Know your level** - Be honest about where you are
3. **Think about your goal** - Core, flexibility, relaxation?
4. **Check your equipment** - Mat, wall, or nothing?
