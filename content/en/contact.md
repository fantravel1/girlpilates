## We'd Love to Hear From You

Girl Pilates is a free resource dedicated to making Pilates accessible to every woman. Whether you have questions about exercises, feedback on our content, or ideas for improvement, we're here to listen.

## How to Reach Us

### General Inquiries
For questions about the website, content requests, or general feedback:
- **Email**: hello@girlpilates.com

### Content Corrections
Notice an error in an exercise description or article? Help us improve:
- **Email**: corrections@girlpilates.com

Please include the page URL and describe the issue you found.

### Partnership & Collaboration
Interested in contributing content, becoming a featured teacher, or exploring partnerships?
- **Email**: partners@girlpilates.com

## A Note on Medical Advice

While we strive to provide accurate, helpful information, Girl Pilates content is for educational purposes only. We cannot provide personalized medical or fitness advice. If you have specific health concerns or injuries, please consult a qualified healthcare provider or certified Pilates instructor.

## Response Time

We're a small team passionate about Pilates education. We read every message and aim to respond within 2-3 business days. Thank you for your patience and for being part of our community!
