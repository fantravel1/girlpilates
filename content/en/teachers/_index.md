## Pilates Masters

Learn from the best by getting to know the instructors who have shaped Pilates practice over the decades.

### First Generation Elders

These extraordinary teachers were trained directly by Joseph and Clara Pilates, preserving and passing on the original method.

- [Romana Kryzanowska](/teachers/romana-kryzanowska/) - Heir to Joseph Pilates' method
- [Ron Fletcher](/teachers/ron-fletcher/) - Brought Pilates to Hollywood
- [Lolita San Miguel](/teachers/lolita-san-miguel/) - One of two people certified by Joseph Pilates
- [Deborah Lessen](/teachers/deborah-lessen/) - Studied with Carola Trier

### Contemporary Leaders

The next generation of teachers who have expanded and evolved Pilates for modern audiences.

- [Mari Winsor](/teachers/mari-winsor/) - Creator of Winsor Pilates
- [Moira Merrithew](/teachers/moira-merrithew/) - Co-Founder of STOTT PILATES

- [Kathy Corey](/teachers/kathy-corey/) - Pilates Historian
- [Amy Taylor Alpers](/teachers/amy-taylor-alpers/) - Co-Founder of The Pilates Center

### Pilates Lineages

The Pilates method has been passed down through different lineages, each preserving aspects of the original work while adding their own perspective:

**Classical/Traditional Lineage**
Maintains the exact exercises and sequences as taught by Joseph Pilates.

**Contemporary Lineage**
Incorporates modern exercise science and rehabilitation principles while honoring the fundamentals.

### Certifying Bodies

Major organizations that certify Pilates instructors:

- **PMA** - Pilates Method Alliance
- **BASI Pilates** - Body Arts and Science International
- **STOTT PILATES** - Contemporary approach
- **Balanced Body** - Education and equipment

### The Legacy Continues

Each teacher in our directory represents years of study, practice, and dedication to the Pilates method. Their work ensures that Joseph Pilates' teachings continue to benefit new generations.
