## A Lifetime of Movement Education

Kathy Corey has dedicated over four decades to Pilates education, developing comprehensive training programs and innovative teaching methodologies that have influenced instructors worldwide.

## Background

Kathy's Pilates journey began with study from first-generation teachers. She combined this classical foundation with extensive research into anatomy, biomechanics, and learning theory to develop her own approach to education.

## Educational Innovations

### Comprehensive Training Programs

Kathy has developed detailed curricula covering:

- Mat work fundamentals to advanced
- Reformer progressions
- Wunda Chair specialization
- Ladder Barrel and auxiliary equipment
- Anatomy for movement professionals

### Teaching Methodology

Her approach emphasizes:

- **Understanding principles** before learning exercises
- **Progressive skill development** in logical sequences
- **Hands-on cueing** techniques
- **Assessment skills** for instructors

## Chair Work Expertise

Kathy is particularly known for her expertise with the Pilates Chair:

- Developed comprehensive Chair curricula
- Created innovative exercises and progressions
- Written extensively on Chair work
- Known worldwide for Chair specialization
