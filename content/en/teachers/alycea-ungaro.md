## Where Clinical Meets Classical

Alycea Ungaro brings a unique combination of physical therapy expertise and classical Pilates training to her practice. As a licensed physical therapist and Romana-trained instructor, she bridges the gap between rehabilitation and fitness.

## Background

Alycea's dual background in physical therapy and classical Pilates gives her a distinctive perspective:

- **Clinical training** - Understanding injury, rehabilitation, and anatomy
- **Classical education** - Trained in authentic Pilates methodology
- **Real-world experience** - Working with diverse populations

## Real Pilates NYC

Alycea founded Real Pilates in New York City, a studio known for:

- Authentic classical instruction
- High standards for instructor training
- Integration of rehabilitation principles
- Celebrity and everyday clients alike
