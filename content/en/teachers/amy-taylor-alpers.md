## Guardian of Classical Pilates

Amy Taylor Alpers, along with her sister Rachel Taylor Segel, co-founded The Pilates Center in Boulder, Colorado in 1990. Their studio and teacher training program have become renowned for maintaining the highest standards of classical Pilates education.

## Training and Lineage

Amy studied extensively with first-generation teachers including:

- **Romana Kryzanowska** - Joseph Pilates' primary protégé
- **Other Pilates Elders** - Learning from multiple lineages

This direct connection to Joseph Pilates' original teaching informs everything at The Pilates Center.

## The Pilates Center

Founded in 1990, The Pilates Center has become:

- A destination for serious classical training
- A leading teacher training program
- A repository of classical knowledge
- A community for dedicated practitioners

## Teacher Training

The Pilates Center's teacher training program is known for:

### Rigorous Standards
- Comprehensive written and practical exams
- Extensive observation and practice hours

- High completion standards

### Classical Foundation
- Authentic exercise execution
- Original sequencing and flow

### Depth of Knowledge
- Detailed anatomy education

- Teaching methodology

## Philosophy

> "Pilates is not about being perfect. It's about striving toward an ideal with awareness, precision, and joy."

Amy's teaching emphasizes:

- **Authenticity** - True to Joseph's original work
- **Rigor** - High standards without rigidity
- **Community** - Supporting practitioners and teachers
- **Continuous learning** - Always deepening understanding
