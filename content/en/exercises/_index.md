### What's Included in Each Exercise

Every exercise in our library features:

- **Clear Instructions**: Step-by-step guidance for proper form
- **Breathing Cues**: When to inhale and exhale
- **Modifications**: Easier and harder variations
- **Common Mistakes**: What to avoid
- **Benefits**: What muscles and skills you're developing
- **Tips for Success**: Expert advice for getting the most from each movement

### Exercise Difficulty Levels

**Beginner**
Perfect for those new to Pilates. Focus on foundational movements, learning to engage your core, and understanding breath patterns.

**Intermediate**
Ready to progress? Intermediate exercises add complexity, longer holds, and more challenging coordination.

**Advanced**
For experienced practitioners. These exercises require strength, flexibility, and precise control developed over time.

### Start Your Practice

Not sure where to begin? Try these classic Pilates exercises:

1. **The Hundred** - The signature Pilates warm-up
2. **Roll Up** - Spinal articulation and core strength
3. **Single Leg Circles** - Hip mobility and stability
4. **Rolling Like a Ball** - Spinal massage and control
5. **Plank** - Full body strength and stability
