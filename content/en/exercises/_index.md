## Featured Exercise Series

### The Classical Mat Order
Joseph Pilates created a specific sequence of 34 exercises designed to flow together:

1. **The Hundred** — Signature warm-up for core activation
2. **Roll Up** — Spinal articulation and abdominal strength
3. **Single Leg Circles** — Hip mobility and pelvic stability
4. **Rolling Like a Ball** — Spinal massage and balance
5. **Single Leg Stretch** — Core endurance and coordination

[View Complete Classical Order →](/exercises/mat/)

### Series of Five (Ab Series)
The famous five-exercise abdominal sequence:
- Single Leg Stretch
- Double Leg Stretch
- Single Straight Leg Stretch
- Double Straight Leg Stretch
- Criss Cross

### Back Extension Series
Build posterior chain strength:
- Swan
- Swimming
- Single Leg Kick
- Double Leg Kick
