Knees to Chest is a simple, calming stretch that releases tension in the lower back and hips.

## Hug and Release

This gentle position helps decompress the lower spine and is perfect for relieving back tension.

## Benefits

- **Lower Back Release**: Decompresses lumbar spine
- **Hip Flexor Rest**: Allows hip flexors to relax
- **Calming**: Promotes relaxation
- **Accessible**: Safe for most people

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Pulling too hard** - Keep it gentle
2. **Tensing shoulders** - Relax upper body
3. **Holding breath** - Breathe deeply
4. **Rushing** - Take your time

## Tips for Success

- Let your lower back melt into the mat
- Keep your head relaxed on the floor
- Rock side to side to massage your back
