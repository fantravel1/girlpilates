Seated Hip Stretch targets the outer hip and piriformis muscle in a comfortable seated position.

## Cross and Stretch

This accessible stretch works well for all levels and can be done almost anywhere.

## Benefits

- **Hip Opening**: Stretches outer hip
- **Piriformis Release**: Targets deep rotators
- **Accessible**: Easy seated position
- **Office Friendly**: Do at your desk

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Rounding spine** - Stay tall
2. **Forcing stretch** - Keep it gentle
3. **Holding breath** - Breathe deeply
4. **Rushing** - Hold for adequate time

## Tips for Success

- Keep your crossed foot flexed
- Lean from hips, not by rounding back
- Breathe into the stretch
