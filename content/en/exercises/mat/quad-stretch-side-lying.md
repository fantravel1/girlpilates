Quad Stretch Side Lying is a comfortable way to stretch the quadriceps muscles in a supported position.

## Pull and Hold

This classic stretch targets the front of the thigh effectively.

## Benefits

- **Quad Flexibility**: Lengthens quadriceps
- **Hip Flexor Stretch**: Also stretches hip flexors
- **Supported Position**: Comfortable for longer holds
- **Balance Counter**: Balances quad-dominant activities

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Arching back** - Keep spine neutral
2. **Pulling too hard** - Gentle is effective
3. **Knee dropping** - Keep knees aligned
4. **Holding breath** - Breathe into the stretch

## Tips for Success

- Keep hips stacked vertically
- Breathe deeply throughout
- Hold for adequate time to see benefit
