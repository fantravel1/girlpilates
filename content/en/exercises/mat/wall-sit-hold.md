Wall Sit Hold is a challenging isometric exercise that builds quadriceps strength and endurance.

## Sit and Hold

This static exercise requires no movement but plenty of muscular engagement.

## Benefits

- **Quad Strength**: Builds thigh muscles
- **Endurance**: Isometric holding builds stamina
- **No Equipment**: Just need a wall
- **Functional**: Mimics sitting/standing patterns

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Knees past toes** - Keep shins vertical
2. **Back arching** - Press flat against wall
3. **Holding breath** - Breathe steadily
4. **Giving up too soon** - Push through the burn

## Tips for Success

- Press your whole back into the wall
- Engage your core throughout
- Focus on steady breathing
