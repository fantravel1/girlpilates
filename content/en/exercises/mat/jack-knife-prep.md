Jack Knife Prep develops the strength and control needed for the dynamic Jack Knife exercise. This preparation teaches the positions in a controlled manner.

## Prepare For Lift Off

This exercise builds the inverted strength needed for Jack Knife while teaching proper alignment and control.

## Benefits

- **Core Strength**: Powers the lift and control
- **Back Strength**: Supports the inverted position
- **Shoulder Stability**: Bears weight in shoulder stand
- **Control**: Develops precise movement

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Rolling onto neck** - Stay on upper back
2. **Using momentum** - Move with control
3. **Holding breath** - Breathe throughout
4. **Rushing** - Take time in each position

## Tips for Success

- Press your arms firmly into the mat
- Keep your core engaged throughout
- Think of reaching your legs to the ceiling
