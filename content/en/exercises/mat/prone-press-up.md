Prone Press Up is a gentle extension exercise often used for back health and mobility.

## Press and Extend

This therapeutic movement helps maintain healthy lumbar spine extension.

## Benefits

- **Spinal Extension**: Promotes healthy back curve
- **Counter to Flexion**: Balances sitting posture
- **Gentle Movement**: Appropriate for many conditions
- **Core Activation**: Stabilizes during movement

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Lifting hips** - Keep pelvis down
2. **Looking up** - Keep neck neutral
3. **Going too far** - Stay within comfort
4. **Rushing** - Move smoothly

## Tips for Success

- Keep movement slow and controlled
- Only go as high as comfortable
- Breathe naturally throughout
