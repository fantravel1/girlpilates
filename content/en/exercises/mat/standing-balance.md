Standing Balance is a fundamental exercise that challenges your stability system. Good balance is essential for daily activities and injury prevention.

## Find Your Center

Balance requires the integration of multiple body systems - visual, vestibular, and proprioceptive. Training balance improves all of these.

## Benefits

- **Balance Improvement**: Directly trains stability
- **Ankle Strength**: Standing leg works hard to stabilize
- **Core Activation**: Core engages to maintain upright posture
- **Fall Prevention**: Better balance reduces fall risk

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Holding breath** - Breathe naturally throughout
2. **Tensing up** - Stay relaxed, allow small adjustments
3. **Looking down** - Keep gaze forward
4. **Giving up too quickly** - Balance improves with practice

## Tips for Success

- Engage your core for better stability
- Press firmly through your standing foot
- Start with support and progress to freestanding
