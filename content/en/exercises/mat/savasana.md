Savasana is the final resting pose that allows the body and mind to integrate the benefits of practice.

## Rest and Integrate

This essential closing position gives your nervous system time to absorb the work you've done.

## Benefits

- **Nervous System Reset**: Activates relaxation response
- **Integration**: Allows body to absorb practice benefits
- **Stress Relief**: Reduces cortisol and tension
- **Mental Clarity**: Clears and calms the mind

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Skipping it** - This is an important part of practice
2. **Too short** - Give yourself adequate time
3. **Tensing up** - Let everything completely release
4. **Planning** - Let go of thoughts about what's next

## Tips for Success

- Cover yourself with a blanket if cold
- Use props to be completely comfortable
- Let go of all effort - just be
