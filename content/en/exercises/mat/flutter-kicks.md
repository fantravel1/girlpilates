Flutter Kicks are an effective exercise for targeting the lower abdominals and hip flexors with continuous movement.

## Kick and Stabilize

This endurance exercise keeps your core engaged throughout the rapid alternating leg movement.

## Benefits

- **Lower Ab Work**: Challenges bottom portion of abs
- **Hip Flexor Strength**: Continuous leg movement
- **Core Endurance**: Builds stamina
- **Simple Setup**: No equipment needed

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Back arching** - Keep spine neutral
2. **Kicking too big** - Small, controlled kicks
3. **Holding breath** - Breathe continuously
4. **Neck straining** - Relax upper body

## Tips for Success

- Press lower back firmly into mat
- Keep kicks small (about 6 inches)
- Focus on continuous, steady movement
