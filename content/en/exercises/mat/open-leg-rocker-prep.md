Open Leg Rocker Prep builds the balance and flexibility needed for the full Open Leg Rocker. This preparation teaches you to balance with open legs before adding the rolling motion.

## Balance in V

This exercise develops the balance point and hamstring flexibility needed for the rocking version.

## Benefits

- **Balance**: Develops seated balance
- **Hamstring Flexibility**: Stretches back of legs
- **Core Control**: Maintains balance position
- **Preparation**: Builds toward full exercise

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Rounding too much** - Keep chest lifted
2. **Gripping legs** - Hold gently
3. **Holding breath** - Breathe naturally
4. **Forcing legs straight** - Work within your range

## Tips for Success

- Find your balance point before extending legs
- Keep your spine long, not collapsed
- Use your core to maintain position
