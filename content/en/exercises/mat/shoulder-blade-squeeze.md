Shoulder Blade Squeeze strengthens the muscles that counteract rounded shoulders and poor posture.

## Squeeze and Hold

This simple exercise targets the middle back muscles often weakened by sitting.

## Benefits

- **Posture Improvement**: Strengthens postural muscles
- **Counter Rounding**: Combats forward shoulders
- **Upper Back Strength**: Targets rhomboids
- **Easy to Do**: No equipment needed

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Lifting shoulders** - Keep them down
2. **Arching back** - Stay tall
3. **Holding breath** - Breathe normally
4. **Quick releases** - Control the movement

## Tips for Success

- Think of squeezing a pencil between shoulder blades
- Keep core engaged
- Perform frequently throughout the day
