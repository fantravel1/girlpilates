Toe Yoga improves foot strength, mobility, and control through intentional toe movements.

## Lift and Spread

This exercise develops the intrinsic muscles of the feet often neglected in traditional training.

## Benefits

- **Foot Strength**: Builds intrinsic muscles
- **Toe Control**: Improves dexterity
- **Balance Foundation**: Strong feet aid balance
- **Injury Prevention**: Healthy feet reduce injuries

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Getting frustrated** - Progress takes time
2. **Tensing body** - Keep relaxed while trying
3. **Skipping practice** - Consistency is key
4. **Giving up** - Even small improvements help

## Tips for Success

- Practice barefoot daily
- Be patient with yourself
- Celebrate small improvements
